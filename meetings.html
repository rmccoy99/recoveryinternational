Telephone Meetings

Are you new to Recovery International?

Newcomer Meeting

  • Free 6-session training course
  • Learn the RI Self-Help Method
  • Each session is 90 minutes long
  • Include a question and answer period
Schedule

Open Literature Meeting

  • 60 Minute Session
  • Focus on reading and discussing Dr. Abraham Low work
  • Great option for people with visual impairment or those who are reading challenged
See Meeting Times

Are you a returning member to Recovery International?

Supplemental Meetings

  • For new members who have completed the Newcomer program
  • Supplemental meetings can be attended anytime throughout the week
  • Participation with readings, examples, and spotting is required
See Meeting Times

Traditional Assigned Meeting

  • Members who have demonstrated a working knowledge and practice of the RI Method
  • Allowed one Traditional Assigned Meeting per week
  • It is required that you attend and fully participate in any Assigned Meeting a minimum of twice monthly
See Meeting Times

Special Focus Meeting

  • For members who struggle with chronic health issues
  • Each member is allowed one Special Focus Meeting per week
  • It is required that you attend and fully participate in any Assigned Meeting a minimum of twice monthly
See Meeting Times

Meeting Rules

CANCELLATIONS

If a meeting cancellation occurs with advance notice we will post a notification above. Please be aware that last minute cancellations may arrises due to emergencies or technical difficulties. If you are unable to join a telephone meeting despite following the current schedule, adjusting to your time zone correctly, and using the accurate phone number and access code, it may indicate that the meeting has been canceled or there are technical difficulties.

If you have any questions please email gilda@recoveryinternational.org.
